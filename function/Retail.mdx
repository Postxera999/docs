## Feature Overview

The Retail module provides users with retail store information and search functionality, displaying exclusive offers based on membership tiers. Users can view store details (brand introduction, operating hours, location, promotional information, available coupons) and perform contact, sharing, and voucher usage functions.

## Shopping Directory

Users enter the Shopping Directory page through the Retail card on the Discover page

The page title is "Retail"

This page includes the following primary areas:
1. Top navigation: contains Filter, Search entry, Sort button, and quick filter tabs.
2. Offer hint area: shows the user's membership level and the benefits they enjoy.
3. Store list: displays retail stores that match the selected filters.

### Top Navigation and Function Area
From left to right at the top of the page:
- Filter: Click the "Filter" button to trigger the filter **popup/drawer**.
- Search entry: Click the search icon located to the right of the search box to enter the store name search page.
- Sort button: Click the "Sort" button to trigger sort options **popup/drawer**.
- Quick filter tabs: To the right of the sort button, display **horizontally scrollable quick filter tabs for store categories**, such as "Beauty & Healthcare". After scrolling and clicking different tabs, the list content will update in real-time to stores of the corresponding category.

- Store Name: Following the logo, display the store's full name, such as "Emperor Jewellery".

	- Click "See more" to ****expand the complete introduction content; click again to collapse.****
- Store Attributes: List display of the store's key information, each piece of information includes a label and corresponding content:
	- Opening Hours: Display operating status and operating hours
		- Operating status: such as "Open Now"
		- Operating hours: "Until 22:00"
	- Category: Display the category the store belongs to, such as "Watches & Jewelry".

	- Accepted Discounts: Display in list format the types of discounts the store accepts, such as "R$ accepted", "Coupon Accepted".
- Contact button: Click the "Contact" button, **specific content and interaction flow that pops up after clicking the Contact button, including whether there are multiple contact methods such as phone, email, or even online customer service**
- Share button: There is an independent "Share" button at the bottom of the page, clicking it **triggers the mini-program's share action.**

#### Contact Modal (Contact)

When users click the "Contact" button on the store detail page, this modal will pop up on the page.

<img src="/images/Retail/Retail-6.png" alt="描述文字" width="300" height="200" />

The card contains the following content:
- Modal title: Display "Contact" at the top left.
- Help text: Below the title, display guidance text such as "We're Here To Help", with specific descriptions like "Have a question or need assistance? Call our 24/7 Guest Service Hotline or connect with us on WeChat.", guiding users to seek help.
- Service hotline: Displayed as an independent module, including:
    - Module title: "Service Hotline".
    - Description: "24/7 service for you.".
    - Phone number: "+853 2888 0888". This phone number is in a clickable button **Is it hardcoded?**
- WeChat customer service: Displayed as an independent module, including:
    - Module title: "Customer Service on WeChat".
    - Description: "24/7 service for you.".
    - QR code: Display a QR code. Below it is the prompt text "Long press QR code to add Galaxy WeChat.", instructing users to add WeChat customer service by long-pressing to recognize or save the image. **Is it hardcoded**

User actions:
- Make a call: Users click the call button for customer service hotline, **which will then invoke the system's phone call function**
- Long press QR code: Long-pressing the customer service QR code will invoke WeChat's scan function, after scanning users can add Galaxy's official customer service WeChat account
- Close modal: Click the top right corner to close the modal. Users can also close the modal by clicking outside the modal area or swiping down.

#### Share Modal (Share)

When users click the "Share" button on designated pages (such as store detail pages), this modal slides up from the bottom.

<img src="/images/Retail/Retail-7.png" alt="描述文字" width="300" height="200" />

The modal provides multiple sharing channels in icon and text format:
- Share to Friends: Click to trigger WeChat mini-program's share to friends function.
- Share to Moments: Click to trigger WeChat mini-program's generate Moments share image function, users can share images containing page information to Moments.
- Save image: Click to save the current page's share poster image to the user's phone album.

User actions:
- Close modal: Click the top right corner to close the modal. Users can also close the modal by clicking outside the modal area or swiping down.

**The specific design and content of share poster images generated by "Share to Moments" and "Save image" functions (such as including store name, address, logo, etc.) need to be confirmed with designers and operations team.**

**Exception handling: When users haven't authorized album access, the interaction prompts and failure handling logic for the "Save image" function need to be supplemented.**

### Coupon Display Area
This area displays coupons provided by the store, with coupons displayed in card format.

<img src="/images/Retail/Retail-8.png" alt="描述文字" width="300" height="200" />

Each coupon card content includes: **Data source**
- Coupon image: Display the coupon's accompanying image.
- Coupon name: Such as "MOP 100 Retail Voucher".
- Applicable scope: Display the coupon's restrictions, such as "Retail only".
- Validity period: Display the coupon's expiration date, such as "Valid until 09.24".

Use button: Each coupon card has a "Use" button on the right side. Clicking the "Use" button **triggers the coupon collection or usage process**.

**Are there other states besides usage? Specific collection/usage logic needs to be defined according to business rules, such as:**
- Uncollected state: Button text is "Collect", clicking will store the coupon in the user's account.
- Collected but unused state: Button text is "Use", clicking will enter the coupon detail page or display QR code/barcode for offline use.
- Used/expired state: Button should be unclickable, text may display "Used" or "Expired".

**Data source: Coupon display and state logic depend on user login status, coupon data in the account, and the coupon's own validity period.**
**How are multiple coupons presented on the page?**

- Benefits list: The module displays multiple membership benefits in grid format, each benefit includes:

### "You May Also Like" Section

<img src="/images/Retail/Retail-13.png" alt="描述文字" width="300" height="200" />

This module displays other stores recommended by the system based on user behavior or store attributes (such as category, location) in a horizontally scrolling card list format.

Each recommendation card must contain:
  - Store Logo: Display the store's brand logo.
  - Store Name: Display the store's full name, such as "Rimowa".
  - Location Information: Display the store's floor and unit number, such as "1037 & 1039 1/F Prode...".
  - Store Category: Display the category the store belongs to, such as "Lifestyle & Gifts".

Each recommendation card may contain:
- Promotional quantity tag: When stores have promotions, display corresponding promotional tags, such as "2 Offers".

User operations:
- Users can swipe horizontally to view more recommended stores.
- Clicking on any recommendation card will jump to that store's detail page.

**The recommendation logic for this module needs to be defined in collaboration with the backend team.**
**The recommendation list should have a reasonable display quantity limit and ensure diversity of recommended content.**
**Data loading: The recommendation list should load asynchronously after page load, with corresponding loading and loading failure UI states.**
