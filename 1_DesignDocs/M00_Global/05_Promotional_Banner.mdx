## **Key Functional Features**

This module presents rebate-related promotions and membership benefits in a dynamic, visually compact format. It allows users to see their own membership tier and rebate entitlement, while also displaying other NG$ rebate rates available to higher or lower tiers for comparison. This encourages awareness of membership benefits and tier progression incentives.

## **Display Logic**
- The module is visible only when the user is logged in. When displayed, it includes:
    - The user’s current membership tier (e.g. “NG Diamond”).
    - The corresponding rebate rate for the current tier.
    - A series of benefit cards showing exclusive rebate rates and promotional offers available to the user.
- Non-logged-in user will not see this section.

## **Layout**
- Single card: displayed at full width.
- Multiple cards: along with other promotional benefit card, each card displayed at 300px width in a scrollable horizontal carousel
